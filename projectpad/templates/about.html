About Us

ProjectPad is a web app designed to simplify lab project management, enabling users to effortlessly create and manage notes for their experiments. With a strong focus on efficient workflow, ProjectPad streamlines lab project management like never before, offering four key features to help users manage their projects more efficiently.


The inspiration for the ProjectPad web app stemmed from my experience as a research scientist who struggled to keep track of my lab notes. As part of Holberton School's foundation in software engineering program, I was tasked with creating a Portfolio Project within a two-week timeline. Thus, I conceptualized and developed ProjectPad to meet the program's requirements while addressing a common problem among lab professionals and students.

ProjectPad is designed to assist individuals involved in lab projects who require a systematic and efficient way to manage notes and related information. The web app caters to a diverse group of users, including students, researchers, lab assistants, and professionals who need to document and organize their lab projects' progress and results. The app's accessibility and convenience benefit users who have difficulty physically accessing their notes or need to collaborate with others remotely. Additionally, the app's efficient project management and streamlined workflow cater to users who have limited time or resources to dedicate to note-taking and organization.

In just two weeks, I successfully delivered the MVP for ProjectPad, and I am proud of the effort and dedication I invested in this project. As a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, ProjectPad showcases my skills in software engineering, project management, and problem-solving, and I look forward to developing it further in the future.
